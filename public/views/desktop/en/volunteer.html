Volunteering in Matsuri

Matsuri's primarily intend is to provide a "place" and "experience" for all to interchange cultural understandings and inspire children, the traditional Japanese values. With Matsuri expanding year after year, we ask for help from the volunteers to run the event with us.

On the day of the festival, not only the members of the ACJ, who works for free, but also many volunteers helped us eveyr year. In 2024, we havd more thant 140 voolunteers, including exchange students from Japan.
